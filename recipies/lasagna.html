Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the sauce with ground beef, garlic, onion, and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 40 minutes.
